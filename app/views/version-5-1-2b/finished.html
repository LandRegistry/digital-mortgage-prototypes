Thank you for signing your mortgage deed

What happens next?

  • If you are borrowing money with other people, they will need to sign the mortgage deed if they haven't already done this
  • Once all borrowers have signed the mortgage deed, the conveyancer will be notified so that they can continue to do the legal work needed for your mortgage
  • Land Registry will update your title register to include the new mortgage when requested by the conveyancer

The time this takes will vary depending on the arrangements between you and your lender and the conveyancer.
