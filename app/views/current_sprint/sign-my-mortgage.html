View your mortgage deed

Use this service to view and check your mortgage details are correct. The online deed is not the legal mortgage. You should still sign and return your paper mortgage deed following the instructions you have from your conveyancer.

Once all parties have signed the paper mortgage your conveyancer will then ensure the Land Registry is updated.

To do this you will need:

  • a unique borrower reference sent to you by your coveyancer
  • the mobile phone you registered with your conveyancer

By using this service you consent to Land Registry retaining your personal details for fraud prevention and detection purposes. You also consent to Land Registry carrying out third party checks in relation to the information supplied.

For further information please view our Personal Information Charter.
